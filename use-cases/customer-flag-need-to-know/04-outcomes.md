---


All figures are directional.

**The front line (reception, store)**
Know only the needed handling category; serve without holding sensitive reasons. Less variance across staff.
*e.g. personal-data holding shifts from a reasoned ledger to handling-category only.*

**Authorized staff / operations**
View the basis only as needed; keep the flag's legitimacy and provenance tamper-free. Less dependence on a single manager.
*e.g. complaint/audit response shifts from verbal explanation to limited disclosure plus a trail.*

**The customer**
The reason doesn't spread unnecessarily; on a subject-access request, the basis can be checked within scope.
