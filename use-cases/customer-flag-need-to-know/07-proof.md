---
title: "What Lemma cryptographically guarantees"
---


- The flag's "handling category" and proof it was set by a legitimate authority
- Authenticity via the setter, time, and basis docHash
- Split selective disclosure across front line / authorized staff / audit
- A trail that authorized staff, audit, and (when required) the subject can check — without exposing the reason to the front line

Note: the validity and lawfulness of flagging itself is the operator's responsibility; Lemma supports minimal disclosure, tamper-resistance, and explainability.
