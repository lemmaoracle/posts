# Use Case: Need-to-Know Customer Flags

## Thesis

**The action travels. The reason doesn't.**

Running internal "handle with care" flags on customers with past trouble forces a dilemma: show the reason to front-line staff and accept leakage and inconsistency, or hide it and lose the ability to show the flag was legitimate. Lemma passes the **handling category** to the front line as a proof, while the basis and history stay with authorized staff via selective disclosure. Who set the flag, when, and on what basis remains tamper-proof as provenance, so complaints, subject-access requests, and audits can be answered while opening the basis only as needed.
