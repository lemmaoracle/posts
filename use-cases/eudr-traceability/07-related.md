### CBAM Supplier Attestation — embedded-carbon attestation
**Reported ≠ provable**

The carbon-border cousin of EUDR. Same multi-tier attestation pattern, applied to country, carbon intensity, and CBAM-specific measurement attributes.

[See the use case →](/use-cases/cbam-supplier-attestation/)

### Supply Chain ESG — verifiable sustainability attributes
**Declared ≠ proven**

The general ESG pattern: tier-by-tier attestation across the supply chain without each supplier disclosing source data to its customer's customer.

[See the use case →](/use-cases/supply-chain-esg/)

### Supply Chain Component Provenance — verifiable parts attestation
**Logged ≠ traceable**

The same pattern applied to mechanical and electronic component provenance — useful when EUDR-covered material flows into manufactured goods downstream.

[See the use case →](/use-cases/supply-chain-component-provenance/)
