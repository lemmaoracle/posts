---
title: "What Lemma cryptographically guarantees"
---


- The principal that delegated the agent, the role granted, and the scope of the delegation
- A per-call spend limit, bounded against the principal's on-chain policy
- A jurisdiction attribute the counterparty needs to match before clearing
- Revocation propagation: a principal-level update invalidates dependent attestations without touching the agent
- An audit-grade record verifiable independently by counterparties, regulators, and internal audit — without ever exposing the principal's signing keys
