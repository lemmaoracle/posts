# Use Case: Long-Term Contract Records, Provable Years Later

## Thesis

**Having a record ≠ being able to prove it**

For 15–20 year maintenance contracts and infrastructure ledgers, prove cryptographically — without revealing the contents — that an amount, spec, or inspection record "legitimately existed and was legitimately amended at that time," even when the question arrives years later.
