### RAG Source Attestation — Citation Verification
**Cited ≠ verified**

Cryptographically guarantees that citations RAG presents as the basis for AI decisions match the original documents. A complementary relationship within the same Pillar as AI Audit Log Proof.

### RAG Content Provenance — Pre-Ingestion Verification
**Indexed ≠ trustworthy**

Fixes document provenance and issuer signatures at the moment of ingestion. The layer that guarantees the grounds of the grounds of a decision.

[View use case →](/use-cases/rag-content-provenance/)

### KYC/AML Selective Disclosure — Prove Without Sharing
**Declared ≠ verified**

Meets regulatory requirements without disclosing customer attributes in financial settings. Combined with AI Audit Log Proof, enables full traceability of "who decided what, about whom, on what basis."
