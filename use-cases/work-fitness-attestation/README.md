# Use Case: Work-Fitness Attestation

## Thesis

**Show fitness, not the health record.**

Where employment requires health checks, training, or qualifications, collecting stool tests or medical results turns that storage into a leakage surface for special-category data. Lemma lets staff present only an issuer-signed proof that the required checks, training, and qualifications are met — medicals and attendance stay with the individual and the issuer, while assignment and audit verify without contents.
