---
title: "What Lemma cryptographically guarantees"
---


- Proof staff "meet the required health checks, training, and qualifications"
- Authenticity via the original's docHash and issuer signature
- Trackability of expiry/withdrawal
- A trail assignment, audit, and third parties verify independently — without exposing health data

Note: lawful handling, consent, and retention of special-category data is the operator's responsibility; Lemma supports minimal disclosure, tamper-resistance, and explainability.
