### Financial Data Exfiltration — cross-org access made tamper-evident with ZK
**Trusted ≠ verified**

When decisions or attributes have to be shared across organizations, the access and verification layer can itself be made tamper-evident with ZK. Stacks cleanly with the predicate proofs here: trust of the decision and trust of the sharing path closed at once.
