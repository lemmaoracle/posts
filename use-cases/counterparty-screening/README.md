# Use Case: Counterparty Screening — Pass the Result, Not the Reasons

## Thesis

**The result travels. The contents don't.**

Counterparty credit and sanctions screening forces a dilemma: share the basis of a decision and you risk leakage and defamation; withhold it and every firm re-checks the same party. Lemma passes the **decision only** as a ZK proof, keeping reasons, scores, and history private. The receiving side — counterparty, auditor, or AI — verifies "meets the bar / flagged" without seeing the contents, and the result cannot be altered after the fact.
