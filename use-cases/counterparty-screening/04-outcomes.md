---
title: "What you get"
---

- **The deciding side (credit, procurement, compliance)**: keep the basis in-house and put only the trust of the decision on the wire. The duplicate-work and explainability load drops.
- **The receiving side (counterparty, group company, auditor, AI agent)**: verify without ever seeing the contents. No need to re-screen the same party.
- **The screened party**: irrelevant personal and transactional information stops travelling.
