---
title: "The business problem"
---

Credit and sanctions screening is an everyday gate in sales and procurement. But the basis of a decision (transaction history, credit data, the reason a party is flagged) is leakage-, defamation-, and interference-prone if shared — and if withheld, counterparties, group companies, and banks all re-screen the same party. The trap: "because we can't show the basis, the decision itself isn't trusted."
