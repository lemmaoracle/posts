### AI Audit Log Proof — a permanent record of the decision
**Audited ≠ explainable**

Preserve the AI decision itself as reproducible cryptographic evidence. Stacks with this page (cryptographic identity of references) to close both "what was seen" and "how it was decided."

[View use case →](/solutions/use-cases/ai-audit-log-proof/)

### RAG Source Attestation — independent verification of cited sources
**Cited ≠ verified**

Bind each AI citation to a ZK proof of the exact docHash of the source version, making the basis of an answer cryptographically traceable. Same idea as this page's decision-time attestation, applied to the citation surface.

[View use case →](/solutions/use-cases/rag-source-attestation/)

### RAG Content Provenance — verification before ingest
**Indexed ≠ trustworthy**

Fix document provenance at RAG ingest time via docHash and issuer signature. Makes the origin of what the AI references independently verifiable before reference.

[View use case →](/solutions/use-cases/rag-content-provenance/)
