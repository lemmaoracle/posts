# Use Case: Let AI Use Internal Documents Without Touching the Raw Data

## Thesis

**Letting AI use it ≠ handing over the raw data**

Keep confidential documents and personal data out of the model and out of any external path, while letting the AI use only the needed facts and attributes — with proof attached. The model never touches raw PII or the original; what the AI actually used stays independently verifiable as a tamper-evident trail after the fact.
