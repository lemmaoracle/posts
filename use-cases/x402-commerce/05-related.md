# Related Use Cases

## Delegated Treasury

**Pillar:** P3 Agent Trust Chain

When a buyer agent's spend authority is delegated from an enterprise, the seller can verify not just the seller's attributes — but the buyer's authority to pay. On-chain spend-control attestations complete the trust chain.

→ [Delegated Treasury](/use-cases/delegated-treasury/)

## Multi-Agent Workflows

**Pillar:** P3 Agent Trust Chain

When an orchestrator agent dispatches a purchasing agent, the delegation chain must be traceable. Lemma proves who authorized the purchase and what scope was delegated at each step.

→ [Multi-Agent Workflows](/use-cases/multi-agent-workflows/)
