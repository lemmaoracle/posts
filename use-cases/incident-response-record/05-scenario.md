---
title: "Worked example"
---


A hotel handles a suspected food-poisoning report the same day. A year later the adequacy is questioned, but records have been updated repeatedly with no way to show the original response untampered.

Had Lemma been in place, "when, by what procedure, and who handled it" was anchored tamper-free at the time. The hotel proves "handled legitimately" without disclosing customer data, opening the basis only as needed, and courts/regulators/auditors verify independently.
