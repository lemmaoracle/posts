# Use Case: Tamper-Proof Incident & Complaint Records

## Thesis

**Prove the response, not the customer's data.**

For complaints and incidents — food safety, injury — the record's survival is not the same as its integrity. Lemma anchors the moment of response cryptographically, fixing "when, by what procedure, and who handled it" tamper-free. The contents (customer data, details) stay in-house, and "we handled it legitimately" can be shown years later while opening the basis only as needed.
