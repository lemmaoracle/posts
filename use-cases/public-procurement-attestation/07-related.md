### KYC/AML Selective Disclosure — the broader financial pattern
**Declared ≠ verified**

The general selective-disclosure pattern for regulated participation. Public procurement is one application; the same shape applies wherever an evaluator must check eligibility without taking custody of source records.

[See the use case →](/use-cases/kyc-aml-selective-disclosure/)

### Supply Chain ESG — verifiable sustainability attributes
**Declared ≠ proven**

When public procurement carries sustainability criteria (carbon, deforestation, ESG), the bidder-side attestation composes naturally with the supply-chain attestation pattern used upstream.

[See the use case →](/use-cases/supply-chain-esg/)

### Supplier Credential Verification — verifiable supplier qualifications
**Listed ≠ verified**

The general pattern for verifying that a registered supplier still meets the qualifications its registration claimed. Useful in long-running procurement frameworks where eligibility drifts.

[See the use case →](/use-cases/supplier-credential-verification/)
