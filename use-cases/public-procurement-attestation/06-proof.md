---
title: "What Lemma cryptographically guarantees"
---


- Certifications, financial thresholds, and reference counts — each bound to the issuer the procurement policy already trusts
- Predicate composition: the bidder's submission is a single ZK proof against the tender's eligibility schema
- Consortium aggregation: prime contractors compose sub-supplier attestations into a single chained proof
- The evaluator verifies eligibility without ever holding the underlying certificates, financial records, or reference letters
- Audit-trail integrity: the verification is independently checkable by an oversight body without re-reading bidder packs
- Revocation propagation: an issuer-side update invalidates the next attestation without rebuilding the tender's audit trail
