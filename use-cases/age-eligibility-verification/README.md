# Use Case: Age & Sale-Eligibility Verification

## Thesis

**Confirm the age, not the birth date.**

For products gated by age or eligibility — alcohol, tobacco, pharmacy — taking a birth date or ID at every check makes that storage a leakage surface. Lemma passes only a proof that the customer meets the age or eligibility to sell to, while the birth date and document stay with the individual and the issuer.
