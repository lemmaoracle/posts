---
title: "Worked example"
---


An e-commerce/unmanned store selling age-restricted goods collects and stores ID images. Those images are a leakage surface, and the legitimacy of the check is hard to show later.

Had Lemma been in place, the customer presents only a proof of "meets the age to sell to," without a birth date or image. Stores and auditors verify without seeing the contents, and the check leaves a tamper-proof trail.
