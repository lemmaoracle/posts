### EUDR Traceability — origin and permit attestation
**Traced ≠ provable**

The deforestation-regulation cousin of CBAM. Same multi-tier attestation pattern, applied to country of origin, harvest date, and permit attributes across the supply chain.

[See the use case →](/use-cases/eudr-traceability/)

### Supply Chain ESG — verifiable sustainability attributes
**Declared ≠ proven**

The broader ESG pattern: tier-by-tier attestation of environmental, social, and governance attributes without each supplier disclosing its source data to its customer's customer.

[See the use case →](/use-cases/supply-chain-esg/)

### Supply Chain Component Provenance — verifiable parts attestation
**Logged ≠ traceable**

For mechanical and electronic supply chains, the same pattern applied to component provenance — origin, build date, and conformance attributes for parts that move through multiple tiers.

[See the use case →](/use-cases/supply-chain-component-provenance/)
