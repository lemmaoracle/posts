---
title: "Worked example"
---


A franchise HQ collects restaurant permits, hygiene, and insurance as PDFs from hundreds of stores. Expiries, reuse, and doctored PDFs slip in, surfacing at audit: "a certificate we believed valid had lapsed."

Had Lemma been in place, each store presents a proof of "holds valid license/hygiene/insurance" without the original. HQ and auditors verify validity without contents, and trace later lapses.
