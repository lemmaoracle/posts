### Long-Term Contract Records — provable years later
**Having a record ≠ being able to prove it**

Prove a contract's amount, spec, or amendment legitimately existed at the time — without revealing the contents — years later. The same "fix it at the time, prove it later" pattern from this page, applied to long-term infrastructure records.
