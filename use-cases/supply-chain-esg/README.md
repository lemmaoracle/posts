# Use Case: Supply Chain ESG Compliance

## Thesis

**Declared ≠ proven**

CBAM, EUDR, and Digital Product Passport mandates require proof of carbon emissions, labor conditions, and country of origin — but current practice relies on self-declarations and paper supplier documents. Multi-tier verification is practically impossible, making regulatory compliance a mass of risk and effort. Lemma chains signed attributes from each supplier, creating a path to prove compliance without data exposure.
